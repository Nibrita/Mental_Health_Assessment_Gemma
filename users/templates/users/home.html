Frequently Asked Questions

Is this platform a substitute for therapy?

No. Our platform supports self-reflection and awareness, but is not a replacement for professional mental health care.

How accurate are the assessments?

They're built using evidence-based screening methods. Results are for self-guidance, not clinical diagnosis.

Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. We protect your data using secure practices and never share without your explicit consent.

Can I talk to a therapist here?

Not yet, but we're working on partnerships to connect users with certified professionals soon.

Who is this platform for?

Anyone seeking more clarity and emotional awareness — whether you're struggling or simply checking in with yourself.
